Annual Piano Competition at BYU-Idaho

The Brigham Young University Annual Piano competition, now in its 14th year, strives to provide university students with performing opportunities, and raise the standard of excellence of young pianists. This is a national competition with no geographic restrictions. Information regarding repertoire requirements, award amounts, how to apply will be announced soon.

The Saturday, April 11th competition will be followed by a Recital and Awards Presentation on Sunday, April 20th, 2020 at 3:00 in the Ruth Recital Hall, and will include performances by the first, second, and third place winners, and must be attended by all Honorable Mention winners. Absences in either category will result in cancellation of the award.
