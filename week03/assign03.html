About Me

Ni Hao (Hello in Chinese)! My name is Liya Wang. I am an Asian from Asia (Apparently XD)
I am a new convert in Taichung, Taiwan where it is famous for its food, people, and culture
around the world. I speak Mandarin Chinese. Be an Asian from Asia at BYU-Idaho, my hobby is
to pop American friends’ bubbles (personal space) a lot, which made my American friends and
American roommates so annoyed by me. (What can I say, we Asians have smaller bubbles than
westerners). One fun fact about me was that I was studying in a pre-medical program at a
college in Taiwan. However, I decided not to be a doctor after I need to dissect some
organs of animals in my anatomy lab. As I was searching for extra careers I could go into,
I decided to come to BYU-Idaho, and I studied in Software Engineering. I would like to become
an independent women engineer one day. I really like to eat Japanese snacks a lot. I also like
to try all kinds of food and snacks around the world while I travel. I love learning new
languages and new cultures. At my apartment, I always have tons of snacks to eat, but I always
look skinny. Please don't be jealous of me. This year, my roommate invited me over her place and
visit her family's ranch. I had fun at that ranch and with this horse. It was my first time patting
a horse in my life. I love it so much.
