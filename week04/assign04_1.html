Our Approach

TeachForTaiwan finds outstanding leaders who commit to expanding
educational opportunity, beginning with at least two years teaching in
an under-resourced public school. Working shoulder-to-shoulder with
students, educators, and community members, corps members go
beyond traditional expectations to support the academic and personal
growth of their students.The impact corps members have in the
classroom fuels a lifelong commitment to their students and shapes
the trajectory of their lives and careers.
