About Teach For Taiwan

TeachForTaiwan is founded in 2012 at Taipei, Taiwan
TeachForTaiwan is an NGO inspired by TeachForAmerica.
TeachForTaiwan addresses the issue of educational inequality
by sending youth leaders to the elementary schools in need and
supporting their leadership through the two-year fellowship.
